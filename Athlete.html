Athletics

I have been competing in athletics since the age of 16. I started off competing in cross country before quickly transitioning to the long jump and triple jump in which I have national school and club medals. However due to a knee injury I switched to sprinting while I recovered and I surprised myself with how well I was doing.

Since then I have been competing in the sprint events from 60m up to 400m with the 200m being my strongest event.


200m (PB) Belfast International 2017

I compete at both national and international level and I have represented Ireland several times including at the CSIT Games, European Team Champs and on the Irish 4x100m relay too.

I plan to return to long jump in conjunction with sprinting over the next few years and I hope to make the transition to competing regularly at international level. I will target the next European Championships and continue to try qualify for major championships with the 4x100m relay squad.
